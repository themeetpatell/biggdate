## Slide 3 — The Solution

**Headline:**
> Replace the swipe with a conversation.

**Body:**
BiggDate starts where every real relationship starts — in conversation.

When you open BiggDate, you don't fill out a profile. You talk to **Maahi** — our AI relationship profiler — who asks you 8 real questions in 7 minutes.

Not: "What are your hobbies?"

But: *"What broke your last meaningful relationship? What would you find out on date 3 that would quietly end it? What do you bring to a relationship that's hard to find?"*

From that conversation, Maahi builds your **Soul Profile** — 40+ psychological fields covering your attachment style, conflict patterns, love language, dealbreakers, and the specific things you offer and need in a relationship.

Every day: **3 matches. Not 300.**
Each one explained. Each one honest about where it works and where it won't.

**Visual direction:** Split screen — messy swipe grid (left) vs. one match card with narrative explanation, harmony signals, and friction point (right).

## Slide 4 — How It Works

**Headline:**
> The BiggDate experience in 60 seconds.

**Step 1 — Talk to Maahi (7 min)**
8 questions. Conversational, not clinical. Detects your relationship patterns mid-conversation and reflects them back.

**Step 2 — Your Soul Snapshot**
Your attachment style. Your readiness score. What you bring. What you need. Most people say it's more accurate than anything they'd have written themselves.

**Step 3 — 3 Daily Matches**
Sealed cards. Reveal when ready. Each comes with a narrative: why this person, specifically, for you.

**Step 4 — Life Preview** *(Premium)*
An AI-generated cinematic narrative of the first year together. Not a horoscope. A psychologically-grounded vision story built from both your actual profiles.

**Step 5 — Maahi Between Dates**
The ghosting. The overthinking. The "I think I like them." Maahi knows your history and responds like a friend who's been paying close attention — not a bot.

**Visual direction:** Vertical timeline, phone mockups showing each step. Breathing animation on Maahi chat interface.

## Slide 5 — Maahi

**Headline:**
> Not a chatbot. Your relationship's third brain.

**Body:**
Maahi is three things in one:

**Profiler** — Extracts your full relational psychology in a 7-minute conversation. 40+ fields. No forms. No dropdowns. Real insight from real questions.

**Matchmaker** — Takes both profiles and writes the emotional case for why these two people. Specific to your actual attachment and their actual love language. Not vibes. Architecture.

**Companion** — Available every day. Knows your patterns. Tracks your emotional history over time. Gets smarter the more you use it. Responds in 2–4 sentences that feel like a friend who's been paying attention — never therapises, never over-explains.

**The moat:**
Maahi's memory system learns you over time. Every session adds signal. Every reflection makes the next conversation more useful. Competitors start from zero. Maahi doesn't.

**Visual direction:** Three overlapping circles (profiler / matchmaker / companion) with Maahi's avatar at the center. Subtle breathing animation.
