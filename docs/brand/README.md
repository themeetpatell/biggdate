# Brand Documentation

This section defines how BiggDate should look, sound, and communicate across product, marketing, social, and lifecycle messaging.

## Documents

- [Brand Book Handbook](./brand-book-handbook.md)
- [Content Strategy](./content-strategy.md)

## Who This Is For

- Founders and leadership shaping positioning.
- Product and design teams writing in-product copy.
- Growth and content teams publishing external content.
- Anyone shipping words, visuals, or campaigns under the BiggDate name.

## How To Use

1. Start with the brand book to align on tone, principles, and identity.
2. Use the content strategy to plan and execute channel-specific content.
3. Revisit quarterly and after major product or audience changes.
