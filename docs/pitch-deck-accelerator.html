BIGGDATE06 / 14
AI Companion06
Maahi

Makes you sharper at dating
— not just better at chatting.

Maahi is not a chatbot wrapped in prompts. It’s a relationship companion with persistent emotional memory, integrated into match context, intro history, and post-date reflection.

  • 16 emotional modes — rejection panic, projection, boundary collapse, repair, decision clarity. Each a distinct emotional contract, not a prompt.
  • Persistent session memory — every conversation enriches the user’s `session_memory`. Six months in, Maahi knows them better than any competitor starting fresh.
  • Grounded in user reality — sees match data, intro history, date debriefs. Advice is specific, not theoretical.
Defensibility

Switching cost grows linearly.

Tomorrow’s better foundation model makes Maahi better without re-architecture. The moat isn’t the model — it’s the per-user memory the model operates on.

Plan Gate

3 / 15 / unlimited sessions per week

Free-tier AI burn is bounded at ~$0.10/user/month. Premium AI cost runs $0.40–0.80/user/month — a 3–5% cost line against $19.99 ARPU.
