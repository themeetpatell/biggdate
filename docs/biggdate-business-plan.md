## The Problem

### Dating Apps Are Failing Their Users — By Design

India's dating app market has grown rapidly, but user satisfaction has not. The core issue isn't the size of the pool. It's the quality of the signal.

**What current apps optimise for:** engagement time, swipe volume, notification-driven re-engagement, and paid feature urgency (Super Likes, Boosts, Roses). These metrics increase revenue. They don't increase meaningful connections.

**What actually predicts relationship success:** attachment security, communication compatibility, shared values architecture, life direction alignment, conflict resolution style. None of these are surfaced by a photo and a 150-character bio.

**The result for users:**
- Average Tinder user spends 90 minutes/day, goes on 1.5 dates/month
- 75% of Indian dating app users report feeling "exhausted" by the experience within 6 months (Statista, 2024)
- Premium features cost ₹4,000–₹8,000/month on leading apps with no measurable improvement in match quality
- Apps designed for "serious relationships" (Aisle, TrulyMadly) have improved UX but haven't changed the underlying matching logic

The people who feel this most acutely are also the people hardest to retain on swipe apps: builders, professionals, high-achieving 22-to-40-year-olds who value their time and have high relational standards. This is our user. And right now, there is no product built specifically for them.

## The Solution

### Psychological Matching, Not Swipes

BiggDate replaces the swipe loop with a conversation.

**Maahi** — our AI relationship profiler — conducts an 8-question onboarding interview that extracts attachment style, conflict patterns, love language, dealbreakers, life direction, and relational self-awareness. From this, a 40+ field Soul Profile is generated automatically. No forms. No dropdowns. 5–7 minutes of honest conversation.

Every day, users receive 3 matches — not 300. Each comes with:
- A narrative explanation of *why* these two people (not a score)
- Specific compatibility signals across values, communication, and life direction
- One honest friction point
- An opening conversation question meaningful to both

For premium users: **Life Preview** — a cinematic AI narrative of what the first year with this match could look like, grounded in both profiles' actual psychology.

Running underneath the entire product: **Maahi as Companion** — available for the moments between matches that every other app ignores. The ghosting. The overthinking. The first date debrief. Maahi knows your profile, tracks your emotional patterns over time, and responds like a friend who's been paying close attention.

**Global expansion opportunity:**

### Phase 2: India Public Launch (Months 4–8)

**Target:** 15,000 registered users, 450 paying subscribers, $5,000 MRR

**Channels:**

**Conversion lever:** Life Preview as paid hook — free onboarding is so compelling that the upsell to Life Preview is natural

**KPIs:** 15,000 users, 3% paid conversion, $5K MRR
