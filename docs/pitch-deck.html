02 · COMPANY PURPOSE
The Problem

Dating apps optimize for engagement.
Users want outcomes.

Low-signal profiles

Five photos and a one-line bio cannot encode whether two adults can build a life together.

Misaligned intent

Casual and serious users compete on the same surface, eroding trust for everyone.

Shallow openers

"hey" is the dominant conversation entry — and the dominant reason matches die in 48 hours.

Calendar debt

Time-rich apps for time-poor adults. Every wrong date is hours, not minutes.

The result is dating fatigue — users churn between apps, lose calendar time and emotional bandwidth, and never progress toward a real relationship.
