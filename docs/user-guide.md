# User Guide

## What BiggDate Does

BiggDate is an AI-assisted dating app for intentional relationships. It uses richer profiles, curated intros, safety controls, and premium relationship tools to help people evaluate fit before starting a conversation.

Public tagline: "See your future, not just a profile."

## Access

1. Open the BiggDate app URL.
2. Confirm you are 18+ and accept Terms/Privacy during signup.
3. Sign up with email and password.
4. Verify email if prompted.
5. Complete Phase 1 and Phase 2 onboarding.
6. Add at least one approved profile photo before full discovery.

## Plans

- Free: core profile, limited discovery, limited Soul Knocks, and messaging after accepted intros.
- Premium: higher discovery and Soul Knock limits, Life Preview credits, and more relationship tools.
- Pro: highest limits and advanced tools for heavier users.
- Add-ons: Boost, Life Preview, Super Like, Read Receipts, Incognito, Profile Review, Spotlight.

## Key Workflows

### Create Your Profile

1. Go to onboarding.
2. Complete Phase 1 and Phase 2 questions.
3. Add identity, location, relationship goals, values, interests, and at least one approved photo.
4. Review AI-generated profile suggestions.
5. Save your profile.

### Discover Matches

1. Open the discovery or matches area.
2. Review profile context and compatibility signals.
3. Request an intro, pass, or use available premium actions.
4. Wait for the other person to respond before messaging.

### Manage Intros

1. Open intros.
2. Review sent and received intro requests.
3. Accept, decline, or pass.
4. If both people accept, start the conversation thread.

### Message Someone

1. Open messages.
2. Select an active thread.
3. Send a message.
4. Use coaching prompts if you need help starting or improving the conversation.

### Use AI Coaching

1. Open Coach or Companion.
2. Ask for profile, messaging, date, or reflection help.
3. Review suggestions before acting on them.
4. Keep private or sensitive details minimal.

### Plan And Debrief Dates

1. Use date concierge to plan a date.
2. After the date, use debrief to reflect.
3. Capture what worked, what felt unclear, and next steps.

### Safety Actions

1. Report concerning behavior from safety or profile actions.
2. Flag Pulse content when it violates community expectations.
3. Block a user when you do not want further contact.
4. Severe safety reports are reviewed same day; standard reports target one business day.

## Empty States

- Discovery: "No matches yet. Finish your profile and check back after the next match refresh."
- Intros: "No pending intros. Send a Soul Knock when a match feels worth exploring."
- Messages: "No messages yet. Accepted intros will appear here."
- Pulse: "No posts yet. Start a thoughtful prompt for the community."
- Billing: "No active plan. Choose Premium or Pro when you are ready to unlock more."

## Common Actions

- Reset password: use forgot password on the login screen.
- Update profile: open profile settings and save changes.
- Change plan: open billing and choose checkout or customer portal.
- Upload photo: use profile photo upload and wait for moderation.
- Flag Pulse content: use the flag action on a post.

## FAQs

### Why can't I message someone?

Messaging usually requires a mutual intro or active thread.

### Why is a feature locked?

Some AI, premium, or boost features require an active plan or one-time purchase.

### Why did my photo not appear?

Photos may require upload success and moderation approval.

### What happens when I report someone?

Reports enter admin review. Severe reports are reviewed same day; standard reports target one business day.

### Can I delete my account?

Account deletion is permanent. Use account deletion in settings where available. Data export is support-assisted until self-serve export ships; target response is within 7 calendar days.
