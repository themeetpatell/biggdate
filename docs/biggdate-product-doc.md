## What BiggDate Is

BiggDate is an AI-powered relationship platform built for people who are done with the swipe. Not because they've given up — because they're paying attention.

The insight behind the product: the problem with dating apps isn't the pool. It's the signal. A photo and a job title tell you almost nothing about whether two people will actually work. BiggDate starts where every meaningful relationship actually starts — in conversation — and uses that to build a psychological profile deep enough to match on what actually matters: attachment style, conflict patterns, love language, life architecture, and what someone genuinely brings to a relationship versus what they need from one.

Three daily matches. Each one explained. Not "78% compatible" with no context — but *why*, specifically, in language that respects your intelligence and doesn't pretend chemistry can be calculated.

---

## The Problem

Let's be honest about what's happening on dating apps right now.

- Tinder and Bumble optimise for engagement, not outcomes. More swipes = more ad revenue.
- Premium features (Super Likes, Boosts, Roses) are designed to create urgency and anxiety, not connection.
- The result: 10,000 swipes, 47 matches, 3 conversations, 1 date. They showed up 22 minutes late, ordered a water, and explained Web3 to you for 45 minutes.

The apps built for "serious relationships" aren't much better. They've added more fields to the form. More prompts. Better photography. They've made the surface nicer without touching the signal quality underneath.

Meanwhile, the people genuinely looking for something real — builders, professionals, emotionally intelligent 22-to-40-year-olds — are burning out. They're exhausted not from lack of options but from lack of *relevance*.

---

## The Solution

BiggDate replaces the swipe with a conversation.

When you first open BiggDate, you don't fill out a form. You talk to Maahi — our AI relationship profiler — who asks you 8 questions over the course of a natural conversation. Not "what are your hobbies." Things like: *What broke your last meaningful relationship? What would you find out on date 3 that would quietly end it? What do you bring to a relationship that's hard to find?*

From those answers, Maahi builds your Soul Profile — 40+ psychological and relational fields covering your attachment style, readiness score, core values, growth edges, and the two or three things you genuinely offer a partner and need in return.

Every day, you get 3 matches. Not 300. 3. Each one comes with a narrative — not a score — that explains the emotional core of the connection, the specific places you'll click, and the one honest friction point you'll need to navigate. No false positives. No pretending compatibility is seamless.

For premium users, there's Life Preview: a cinematic AI-generated narrative of what the first year together could actually look like. Not a horoscope. A grounded, psychologically-informed vision story that references your actual attachment patterns and their actual love language and what happens when those two things meet.

And running underneath all of it: Maahi as your companion — available between matches for the moments that dating apps completely ignore. When you've been ghosted. When you're in your head after a first date. When something good happened and you just need someone to tell. Maahi knows your profile, knows your patterns, and responds like a friend who's paid close attention — not a therapist, not a bot.

## Core Features

### Maahi Onboarding
An 8-question conversational interview that extracts your relationship psychology without ever using clinical language. No "attachment style" dropdowns. Just real questions, real answers, real insight. Takes 5–7 minutes. Produces more signal than 3 hours of profile-filling.

**Questions cover:**
- Location and intent (why you're here, what you actually want)
- Gender preference and age range
- Last meaningful relationship — what broke and why
- How you know someone cares (love language, behaviourally)
- Dealbreakers (the ones you won't say out loud on a first date but always matter)
- What you bring (self-awareness as a filter — people who can't answer this clearly rarely do well here)

### Soul Snapshot
After onboarding, your profile is revealed to you. Your attachment style, readiness score (0–100), core values, growth edges, strengths, and the two things you offer and the two things you need. Most people find this more accurate than anything they'd have written themselves.

### Daily Matches
Three matches per day, sealed until you choose to reveal them. Each match includes:
- **Narrative intro** — the emotional logic of why these two people
- **Compatibility signals** — specific observations on values, communication style, and life direction alignment
- **Friction point** — one honest thing that will need attention
- **Opening question** — a shared question both people would answer differently but find meaningful

### Life Preview *(Premium)*
A full AI-generated narrative of a relationship with this match: the first year arc, a Tuesday snapshot, a compatibility map, the hard truth, and a growth score. References your actual profile data — not generic romance language. $2.99 per preview, $5.99 for three.

### Maahi Companion
Your ongoing emotional support between matches. Knows your psychology. Available for the full dating emotional arc — not just the app experience. Detects emotional tone (anxious, spiraling, avoidant, heavy, excited) and responds accordingly. Maintains memory across sessions. Two to four sentences max — never over-explains, never therapises.

### Debrief
Post-date reflection: three questions about chemistry, surprise, and decision. Maahi returns personalised insights and growth notes based on your history. Turns dates into data that makes the next one better.

### Profile Editor
Six tabs: Basics, About, Dating, Lifestyle, Gallery, Visibility. Privacy controls per field (age, city, work, education). Up to 6 photos. Three custom prompt cards generated from your onboarding conversation. Profile visibility modes: Visible, Paused, Hidden.

## Maahi — The AI Core

Maahi is not a chatbot. It's a three-role AI system with a consistent personality and a deep awareness of who you are.

**Role 1: Profiler** — Conducts onboarding. Asks real questions. Detects patterns mid-conversation and surfaces them ("You've mentioned loyalty twice now — that's not an accident"). Never repeats questions. Knows when a profile is complete.

**Role 2: Matchmaker** — Takes your 40+ field profile and generates matches with full psychological justification. Doesn't match on interests or keywords. Matches on relational architecture: how you attach, how you conflict, what you need, what you offer, where life is heading.

**Role 3: Companion** — Your between-dates emotional context. Mirrors your emotion, adds one insight, asks one question. Knows not to say "I hear you" or "That must be hard." Maintains a rolling memory of your emotional patterns, triggers, reassurance style, and recurring themes. Gets more useful over time.

## User Journey

```
Open App
    ↓
Talk to Maahi (8 questions, ~7 min)
    ↓
Soul Snapshot Reveal (attachment style, readiness, values)
    ↓
Dashboard (3 sealed daily matches)
    ↓
Reveal a match → Read narrative → Send Intention (or pass)
    ↓
Life Preview (optional, premium) → Full year narrative
    ↓
Date happens
    ↓
Debrief → 3-question reflection → Maahi insight
    ↓
Talk to Maahi (between dates, between sessions)
    ↓
Repeat daily
```
