About BiggDate - Dating App for Founders & Startup Professionals

Our Mission

BiggDate is the premier dating app designed specifically for founders, entrepreneurs, and startup professionals. We believe that meaningful relationships are built on shared values, vision, and ambition. Our platform connects ambitious professionals who are looking for more than just casual dating - they're seeking their Co-Lifer.

Founded by TheMeetPatel

BiggDate was created by TheMeetPatel, an entrepreneur who understands the unique challenges and opportunities that come with building a startup. After experiencing the difficulties of finding meaningful connections in the fast-paced startup world, TheMeetPatel decided to create a platform that would bring together like-minded professionals who share the same drive and ambition.

Why BiggDate for Founders?

  • Pitch-First Dating: Every profile begins with a pitch, not just photos
  • Verified Professional Profiles: All members are verified professionals
  • Startup Community Focus: Built specifically for the entrepreneurial community
  • AI-Powered Matching: Advanced algorithms match based on compatibility and shared goals
  • 3-Level System: Pitch → Reveal → Journey progression

Our Community

BiggDate brings together founders, entrepreneurs, startup professionals, investors, and business leaders from around the world. Our community is built on the principles of ambition, innovation, and meaningful connections.
